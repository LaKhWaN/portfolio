# The Future of OpenClaw: Founder Joins OpenAI, China's Kimi Makes Its Move

**In a landmark move for the open-source AI community, OpenClaw founder Peter Steinberger has joined OpenAI to lead the next generation of personal agents.**

This is the single biggest story in the agentic AI space this year. For developers building on OpenClaw, it answers critical questions about the project's future and cements its importance in the ecosystem. But the story is bigger than just one hire.

## What This Means for the OpenClaw Community

The immediate question on every developer's mind was: is this the end of open-source OpenClaw? The answer, thankfully, is a definitive **no**.

- **OpenAI Sponsorship**: OpenAI has made "strong commitments" to sponsor the project.
- **The OpenClaw Foundation**: Steinberger is working to establish a proper foundation to govern the project.
- **Dedicated Time**: This move allows the project's founder to dedicate his full time to its development with the backing of the industry's biggest player.

This dual adoption by both a US industry leader and a top Chinese firm solidifies OpenClaw's position as the de-facto standard for building AI agents in 2026.
